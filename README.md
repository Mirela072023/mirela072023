## 🌿 About Me

I'm a **Product Designer** based in Ibadan, Nigeria, focused on creating intuitive digital products that solve real user problems and drive business outcomes.

I specialize in end-to-end product design, from discovery and user research to wireframing, prototyping, and developer-ready Figma handoff. My work spans **wellness, finance, banking, e-commerce, education management systems,** and other digital experiences where usability, clarity, and impact matter.

With a background in business operations, workflow optimization, and systems thinking, I approach design beyond aesthetics—connecting user needs, business objectives, and product strategy to create experiences that are functional, accessible, and meaningful.

When I'm not designing, I'm building systems in Notion, documenting design decisions, exploring emerging technologies, and continuously refining my craft.

> *"Good design isn't decoration — it's clarity, trust, and empathy made visible."*

## 📐 My Design Process

```
Discovery → Define → Ideate → Design → Test → Handoff
```

1. **Discover** — User research, stakeholder interviews, competitive audit
2. **Define** — Problem framing, user personas, journey mapping
3. **Ideate** — Sketching, information architecture, wireframes
4. **Design** — High-fidelity UI in Figma, micro-interactions, responsive variants
5. **Test** — Usability testing, iteration, design review
6. **Handoff** — Dev-ready specs, component documentation, design system
